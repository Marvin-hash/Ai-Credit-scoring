💡 Follow these tips to improve your credit score and increase your chances of loan approval!

📊
Keep Business Records

💰

Tax compliance is one of the strongest indicators of financial responsibility. Compliant businesses score up to 15 points higher.
📈
Grow Your Turnover
Higher business turnover relative to expenses signals a healthy, profitable business. Focus on growing revenue while managing costs.
❓
Frequently Asked Questions
How is my credit score calculated?
Our AI model uses your business financial data — income, turnover, expenses, mobile money usage, and financial behaviour — to calculate a score from 0 to 100.
Do I need a CRB history?
No! CreditIQ uses alternative data so anyone can get scored, even without a formal credit history.
Is my data safe?
Yes. Your data is encrypted and never shared with third parties without your consent.
How long does scoring take?
Less than 1 second! Our AI model processes your data instantly.
